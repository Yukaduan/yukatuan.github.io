Paragraph

They just sit in front of TVs and computers and watch all the information and entertainment spread there until they die, living aimlessly like this. Whether eating or taking a bath, working or falling in love, it’s all just done. They pass the time unconsciously, but still lament how short life is. They live off free entertainment, accompanied by television and the Internet. A wealth of information and a monotonous life can only generate arbitrary ideas and hatred. ——“The Demon King” Kotaro Isaka

Citation

The “citing” element represents content cited from another source, optionally within a footer or cite element, and can also be changed inline with notes and abbreviations.
